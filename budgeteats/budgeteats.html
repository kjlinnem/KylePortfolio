Sloppy Joe's Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 medium red onion diced
  • 1/2 cup BBQ sauce
  • 1/3 cup ketchup
  • Hamburger buns
  • Sliced cheese and pickles for toppings

Instructions:

  1. Brown beef and onions until beef done.
  2. Combine BBQ sauce and ketchup wth beef.
  3. Simmer ingredients for 5 min on low heat.
  4. Scoop portion onto hamburger buns.
  5. Add sliced cheese or pickles for added flavor,

Smoked Sausage and Rice Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 tbs olive oil
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 2 cups cooked rice (follow directions on box)
  • 1 lb smoked sausage
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup shredded cheese

Instructions:

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add butter and olive oil until melted.
  2. Add the sliced smoked sausage and cook for about 5 minutes until sausage begins to brown.
  3. Add the broccoli and cover. Reduce heat to low and cook about 5 minutes more - stirring occasionally.
  4. Add the cooked rice, garlic powder and onion salt to the skillet. Stir well.
  5. Add the shredded cheese - string well until the cheese is melted.
  6. Remove from the heat and serve warm.
